Why use our Go Tennis System service?

We offer a bespoke unique service. We do things digitally and at your pace. With our service we ask for a small donation that will be shared with a dogs charity and animal rescue charity, as part of our aim to give something back.

We offer you the chance to upload your videos for coaching, tips and honest constructive feedback, this in turn will allow you to create your own video diary as well as improve and fine tune your skills. When we say we play the same game, just easier we mean at your pace and time scale. We cater for all ages as well as all levels, so if you’re a beginner that would like to play like a pro, come and give us a go, you will be glad you did.

About Us

How we can help you?

As well as our bespoke and unique service that we offer we can enable you to learn how you want to learn, wherever you want to learn, at a pace that suits you. Our Go Tennis System will allow you to have a virtual one on one and get the most out of your focused and in-depth learning. We focus on you, so you can focus on your improvement, we train when you’re able to train. We set challenges to help you get the most from what you have been taught, as well as put this into practice to show off what you have learned. You’re in control from start to finish.

Coach Adam Jameel

Adam has coached two players who have played professionally, including a Grand Slam junior doubles champion and a Wimbledon men’s doubles champion. He has been sponsored by Babolat, one of the worlds leading brands in tennis equipment since 1875. He continues to coach players of all levels at Abbeydale Tennis Club in Sheffield UK and is fuelled by those who’s goal it is to get themselves to a higher level. Adam is obsessed with tennis and is driven to learn new ways of how to coach in an achievable manner for players of all levels.
